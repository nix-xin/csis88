====== Lab 1 ======

===== Performing a Man in the Middle Attack =====

==== Overview ====
	In this lab, you will learn the fundamentals of a MITM attack. This demonstration will help you understand the background processes at work.
	
		   **Scenario: **An unsuspecting user connected to a free wifi spot is browsing a web server on a local network. Your goal is to redirect the user's traffic to the web site by 
	spoofing the arp requests from the user and web server.


==== Requirements ====
	**wireshark** - Use your knowledge of WireShark to read TCP traffic.
	**arpspoof** - redirects arp packets from a target host (or all hosts) on the LAN intended for another host on the LAN by forging ARP replies.
