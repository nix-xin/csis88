====== Assignment 10 ======

===== Fine Reports for Fine Security Assessments =====

==== Overview ====
	Documentation is an important part of any project. Pentesting applications or systems is no different. 
	Your clients will want to understand at a technical level how you were able to breach their assets so they can make the necessary decisions to fix them. 
	This assignment will focus on your writing and communication skills. 
	

==== Prerequisites ====
	Intermediate writing and reporting skills. A modest approach to communicating ideas.
	View this YouTube Video: [[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qNLMuls2BBA|Public Penetration Test Reports - Learning Resource]]
